Philip's Life and Future

Before I begin, I think it is important to begin with a little background. I hail from Charlotte, North Carolin and come from a family of five, in between two sisters. I am grateful to have grown up in a family that supports and loves me, which has helped me so much in getting to where I am today. Growing up, I fell in love with physical activity. I love to get outside, whether that means playing sports or some other type of outdoor activity, like hiking. In high school, I realized I was an extrovert and spent lots of time hanging out with my buddies, and making new friends. I fell in love with Washington and Lee when I visited, and chose to come because I felt the community and academic setting would allow me to thrive. Now, as I'm finally here after months of anticipation, I look toward the future as I consider where I want to go with my life.
